What is DaybyDay?

DayByDay is a life tracking app. Take a couple minutes out of your life a day to input how your day went. What were the highlights of your day? what were some low points? Rate your day 1-10. Tracking how you do on a daily basis is the first step to help you being a better you. Reflect on your past days and see what made you happy, or what made you sad. Hope you enjoy! more updates to come!
