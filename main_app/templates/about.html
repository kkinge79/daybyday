What is DaybyDay?

DayByDay is your personal life tracking app. It only takes a couple minutes to fill out a day! What were the highlights of your day? what were some low points? Rate your day on a scale from 1 being a really bad day to 7 being an amazing day.

Depression is the leading cause of disability worldwide. Mental illnesses can affect people of any age, race, religion, or income. It is a medical condition that disrupts a persons thinking, feeling, mood, and ability to relate to others and daily functioning. My hopes is with this app you can will be able to get a better understanding of your emotions to help you be a better you. Hope you enjoy! more updates to come!
